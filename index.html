Dessert

The word dessert is derived from the French word desservir, which translates to “to clear the table.” Dessert is a meal which consists of sweet foods. Biscuits, cakes, cookies, custards, ice creams, pastries, pies, puddings, macaroons, etc are identified as dessert in one word.

Tasty Desserts

Most of the time we hear for a healthy lifestyle requires cutting out dessert. But it has some health benefits.

BENEFITS:

  • Balanced Diet
    We can receive a daily amount of required nutrients from fruit pie and smoothies. Cheesecakes, ice cream has valuable amounts of protein and calcium.
  • Mood Booster
    Chocolate reduces stress. Dark chocolate can prevent cancers, high blood pressure.
